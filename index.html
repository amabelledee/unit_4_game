Crystal Counter

Instructions:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are 4 crystals, each representing a different number. By clicking on each crystal, you will add a certain amount of points to your score.
  • Until you click on a crystal, the value will be hidden.
  • To win, you must match the random number exactly. If you exceed the number, you lose.
  • The game restarts each time you win or lose.
